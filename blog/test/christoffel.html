② Parallel Transport: Why Christoffel Symbols Matter

A vector is "parallel transported" along a path. On a curved surface, it rotates even though it's trying to stay parallel! The Christoffel symbols tell us exactly how much correction is needed.

∇ⱼ vⁱ = ∂ⱼvⁱ + Γⁱⱼₖ vᵏ = 0   ← "keep the vector parallel"

Click to set start point and direction. The geodesic equation uses Christoffel symbols to find the "straightest" path in curved coordinates. On a sphere, geodesics are great circles!
