# From Stone Age Tools to ChatGPT

A digital exploration of the history and mechanics of Artificial Intelligence. 

## 📖 About this Project
This project is an attempt to bridge the gap between historical developments from all kinds of sources (like the history of the universe, early use of fire and tools, the development of machines, development of math fom earliest tally bones to modern statistics and geometry) required to now arrive at the modern architecture of Large Language Models. It is structured as a digital textbook that combines conceptual explanations with interactive browser-based visualizations.

The goal is to provide a more intuitive understanding of how complex AI systems have evolved and how they process information today.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
The project is built entirely for the web, using:
- **Vanilla JavaScript** for core logic.
- **Three.js & ECharts** for data and spatial visualizations.
- **TensorFlow.js** for in-browser demonstrations.
- **Temml** for mathematical notation.
